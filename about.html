Who We Are

Founded in 2012 in Dhaka, Hospitech Associates Ltd. has grown into a trusted name in the healthcare supply industry. Our mission is to make advanced medical technology accessible across Bangladesh through reliable imports and distribution.

Our Mission

To deliver quality medical solutions that empower healthcare providers to achieve better patient outcomes.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable and innovative medical equipment supplier in Bangladesh, bridging global healthcare technology with local needs.

Our History

  • 2012: Established Hospitech Associates Ltd. in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
  • 2014: Began importing diagnostic reagents and laboratory consumables.
  • 2016: Formed partnership with Rapid Labs Ltd. (UK).
  • 2019: Expanded operations with Neo Medica (Serbia).
  • 2022: Opened new office at Baitush Sharaf Complex, Airport Road, Dhaka.
  • 2025: Continuing to serve healthcare providers across Bangladesh with dedication and innovation.

Leadership

Md. Moinul Islam – Managing Director. With over 15 years of experience in the medical import sector, he leads our team in strategic growth and international partnerships.
