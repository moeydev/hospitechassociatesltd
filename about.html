About Hospitech Associates Ltd.

Founded in 2012, we have been committed to bringing high-quality medical equipment and laboratory consumables to Bangladesh. Our partnerships with international leaders like Neo Medica and Rapid Labs ensure reliable products and excellent service.

Our Timeline

  • 2012 – Company established in Dhaka, Bangladesh
  • 2015 – Partnership with Rapid Labs (UK)
  • 2018 – Partnership with Neo Medica (Serbia)
  • 2023 – Expanded product line and services nationwide
